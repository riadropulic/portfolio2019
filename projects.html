basic content

Probably the most fun theme for me was the basic content. I enjoyed learning about concept development, recording videos, film, and dramaturgy in media elements as well as editing techniques. As a film lover, I found it fun to work with PremierePro and do some acting for the first assignment of the theme. In a very limited amount of time, we had to tell a story in two minutes without speaking. It was important how we used the composition of the image and angles to show dramaturgical elements. We came up with a funny love story in the library. The second assignment was more complexed, the final product was an informative web page about cycling in Denmark. Our target group were tourists and young people who just moved to the country.

basic ux

For the first time, we used the design sprint method in a group project. The theme consisted of two assignments of which the first one was based on making the final coded prototype and the second one resulted with making a prototype of a native app. Design sprint seemed to be intense in the begging, in five days we came up with the prototype after considering a lot of our different ideas and opinions. This prototype was individually coded later on.
We ended the semester with our final project in which we made a prototype for an app that would help us improve the experience of the global welcoming week of the Copenhagen school of design and technology. The app was developed by focusing on the idea of helping the environment with reducing the paper usage as during this week a lot of paper is used to provide much different information for the participants.
